# Contributors

The Future Friendly Glossary is a community project.
We credit contributors collectively rather than with per-entry bylines: glossary entries are living, collaborative definitions, and authorship evolves as people draft, correct, and review them.
For why it works this way, see [adr-decisions/0007](adr-decisions/0007-collective-contributor-attribution.md).

How credit works here:

- **Git history** is the authoritative record of who contributed what, and when.
- **This page** recognizes contributors and reviewers by name, with their consent.
- Each entry's `contributors` frontmatter tracks who has worked on it.

If you have contributed and want to be added, removed, or listed differently, open an issue or note it in your pull request.
Contributors and reviewers are listed only with their permission.
