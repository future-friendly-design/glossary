# Glottolog

## Definition
Glottolog is a free online catalogue of the world's languages, families, and dialects, each given a stable identifier called a glottocode.

## Why it matters
Glottolog provides an openly accessible, versioned catalogue of languoids (families, languages, and dialects), assigning each a unique glottocode and arranging them in a genealogical tree based on historical-comparative research. It also carries a large bibliographic database of grammars, dictionaries, and word lists, especially for lesser-known languages. Because it is free and citable, it is widely used as a stable alternative or complement to [ethnologue](ethnologue.md).

## Example
A researcher can cite a small language by its glottocode (for example, stan1293) so others can find the exact same entry.
